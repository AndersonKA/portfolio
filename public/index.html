About
Hey, I'm Kae! My first introduction to coding was in 1998 when my best friend and I made (terrible) *N Sync fan websites on Angelfire. I loved making them, but I got a degree in Anthropology because people are fascinating!

I spent the next ten years working in offices after I figured out I wasn't going to be the Indiana Jones. When I started looking for a career change I remembered how much I'd enjoyed coding. I decided to enroll at The Iron Yard, a 12-week immersive coding bootcamp, to learn how to make websites for real.

When I'm not coding I nerd out about: traveling (I've been to 16 countries!), space!, reading (I own more than 500 books), interior design (my style is Jungalow meets Joanna Gaines), and watercolor painting.
