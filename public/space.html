Hey, I'm Kae!

I have a degree in Anthropology (because people are fascinating), but I wasn't planning on being the next Indiana Jones, so I have ten years administrative experience working at some great companies.

I love being useful, so my experience in multiple departments means whether you're in healthcare, tech, marketing, event planning, or accounting I've got you covered. My previous positions have taught me how use use my organizational skills and natural focus and self-motivation to be a great employee.
