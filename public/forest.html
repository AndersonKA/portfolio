>> About <<
Hey, I'm Kae!

I’m a life long learner and a career changer. After spending five years in my last role I was ready for something new, so I left my job and completed the Front-End Web Development program at The Iron Yard. My first introduction to coding was in 1999 when my best friend and I made (terrible) *N Sync fan websites on Angelfire. I’d been interested in coding ever since, but I thought it was something that only people who were really good at math could do!

While I was at The Iron Yard I also started learning about UX design. I have a degree in anthropology, so the combination of social science and web development meets in the middle with UX. I’ve been taking classes online in my spare time to learn more about it.

I’m currently doing two internships: one is for an athletic recruitment startup called Axcess Coaching, where I’ve been helping them build and update their site (which is on WordPress), and the other is for a lifestyle/adventure blog called Kaizening where I’ve been helping with everything from UX to beautifying signup and contact forms to improving SEO.

I love being useful, and my experience in multiple departments means whether you're in healthcare, tech, marketing, event planning, HR, or accounting I've got you covered. My previous positions have taught me how use use my organizational skills and natural focus and self-motivation to be a great employee.
